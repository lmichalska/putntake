Rules

  1. Payment must be made before the start of fishing
  2. After catching a fish of a certain weight for the first time, you can buy bronze, silver and gold pins for DKK25.
  3. Bronze pin: Rainbow Trout over 5 kg, Golden Danish Trout over 2.5 kg, Brook Trout, Brown Trout or crosses over 1.5 kg.
  4. Silver pin: Rainbow Trout over 6 kg, Golden Danish Trout or Brook Trout over 3 kg, Brown Trout or crosses over 2 kg.
  5. Gold pin: Rainbow Trout over 7 kg, Golden Danish Trout or Brook Trout over 3.5 kg, Brown Trout or crosses over 2.5 kg.
  6. Pre-feeding and fishing with live bait is strictly prohibited. Earthworms, flies, small spinners, flashes, corn, and power bait are allowed.
  7. Extra fishing rods must face clearly away from the water.
  8. Fish may NOT be caught with nets.
  9. Fish are cleaned at the cleaning site and waste is placed in a waste container.
  10. Note Change: Catch limitation in the small lake - max. two fish per rod.
  11. Dogs are led on a leash.
  12. Be considerate of other fishermen.
  13. Clean up after yourself.
  14. The car is welcome to be taken around the lakes. When it is wet, you must park in the car park.
