About us

Nørreris Put&Take is located in the picturesque Sydhimmerland, just 600 meters from Hjarbæk Fjord. Our site features three fishing lakes: a large lake with rainbow trout, a children's lake with small trout, and a special lake with large rainbow and golden trout, perfect for fly fishing.

We offer a family-friendly environment with picnic tables, shelters, and facilities for grilling. The area is accessible for people with disabilities. We can also host school trips, bachelor parties, family days, and birthdays in our pavilion, which accommodates up to 60 people.
