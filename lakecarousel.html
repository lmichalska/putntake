The Kid's Lake

Everyone can fish in the small lake. Children under 12 who pay a reduced... price may only fish in this lake. There is a catch limit of 2 fish per rod per paid hour in the small lake.

There is also a rich wildlife in the area, so if not everyone is interested in fishing, you can go exploring in nature and have some good experiences there. The area is approximately 4 ha, of which the lakes cover 1.5 ha, so there is plenty of space for development.
