Our Facilities

We have a lot more to offer than just lakes!

We offer a family-friendly environment with picnic tables, shelters, and facilities for grilling. The area is accessible for people with disabilities. We can also host school trips, bachelor parties, family days, and birthdays in our pavilion, which accommodates up to 60 people.

You can rent a fishing rod for DKK 50 per day. We also sell powerbait and other fishing equipment.
To rent/purchase call us.

Big Pavilion

In the pavilion there is room for approx. 60 people, so the entire school class with parents and siblings or the entire company's staff association can enter the shelter. It can also be rented for events with dining.

Shelter/dining cabins

Several cabins are set up around the lakes. In these you can seek shelter from the wind or eat the food you have brought. From 4 to 10 people can sit in the cabins.

Payment cabin

This cabin is located right between the parking lot and the lakes. Here you will find information, payment envelopes, fridge/freezer and tables where you can eat in the shade but still see the beautiful surroundings.

Cleaning site

Covered cleaning area where you can clean the fish in running water. Should it have been dark, there is light. You can also weigh and measure the fish here.

Toilet

The toilet with heating and a disabled ramp is located together with the other facilities close to the car park.

Shelter

Free shelter for you to enjoy and stay overnight! No fishing necessary!
